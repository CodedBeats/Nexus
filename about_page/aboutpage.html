About Nexus

Project Nexus is team 4's project to create an interactive gaming hub website, named Nexus, that provides two primary functions: allowing developers to upload their web-based mini games (currently W.I.P), sharing them to others; and categorizing and promoting these games for gamers to quickly browse and play the games.
The mini games are light, quick and free, which align with our purposes to open the door for every kind of gamers. This differentiates us with high-profile games that demand high PC requirements, time spent, and purchase cost.
